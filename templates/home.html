Twende

Enjoy the ride!

Twende is a mobile app to improve the informal transport sector by directly connecting drivers and customers. Easily order a Boda Boda through the app and see the driver arrive on your doorstep. You can see reviews and ratings so that you can select a driver that you can trust so that you can sit back and relax. Download the app for free on the app store. Available soon!

Together we make Boda Boda's safer.

You want to go somewhere quick, but there is no available Boda Boda driver. If there is you don't know if this is a safe driver. It's a gamble to get on a Boda Boda. In Twende it is easy to pick a driver that is reliable. Instantly you see the rating and distance of the driver. You can rate, review and give a tip to the driver when you think it was a safe ride. In this way we make the world a safer and a happier place!
